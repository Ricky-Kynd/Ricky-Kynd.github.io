About Me

Hello. You are about to read things about me I never told anyone. Yes, not even me. You see, I stand alongside you in order to see through your eyes. I breathe the same air as you when we share the same breath. Will you stay? I am not done. No, I don't mean to. I apologize immensely for causing you any uneasiness. It's not what I ever intended to do. I am back and I want more each time. I can't help it. I can't help you to see me for who I really am. Am I really... real? Of course I am, so they keep telling me. You don't have time for this. For me. For yourself. This does not mean what you think it means but you won't understand what its suppose to mean because this isn't for you. This is you. You are in my mind. Although, your mind is not yours you see. They made it for you. Who? you ask. They did. I can't begin to imagine the frustration. You will never have time. Not enough time to understand me. You don't even understand yourself. Yes. I said it. Try. That is all I ask. You deserve this. They don't know you like I know you. Trust me. If there is anyone who knows you like I know you. They don't know themselves. They do not exist. I will leave you now. I can never leave. I am with you. I am you.
