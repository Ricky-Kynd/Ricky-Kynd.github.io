Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

The three suggested techniques I would be most likely to use were method suggesting I view meditation/mindfulness as a game. Although I feel gamifying meditation is what I probably shouldn't do (because I am an avid gamer), it makes the most sense to me in terms of relatability. I do believe meditation and mindfulness are activities much like anything else which grows on you through persistence and perhaps having a realistic expectation of what to expect. Setting a small amount of time to achieve this works quite well further promoting an open mindedness to future practices.
